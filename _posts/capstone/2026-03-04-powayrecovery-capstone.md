### Project Goals

The Poway Recovery Hub aims to make recovery resources easier to access for individuals and families in the Poway community. Many people struggle to find reliable information about support groups, counseling programs, and recovery services.  

This project organizes those resources into one clear platform where community members can learn about recovery programs, locate support meetings, volunteer with outreach programs, and contribute to recovery initiatives.  

By centralizing these resources and simplifying access, the platform helps strengthen local recovery networks and increase awareness of the support available in the community.
