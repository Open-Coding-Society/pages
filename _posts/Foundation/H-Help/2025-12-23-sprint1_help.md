### Week Card Icons
- **Circle with "W"** - Week indicator showing the week number
- **✓ Check mark icon** - Indicates a completed lesson or assignment
- **○ Empty circle** - Lesson/assignment not yet completed
- **<i class="fas fa-code"></i> Code icon** - Represents a lesson
- **<i class="fas fa-file"></i> File icon** - Represents an assignment

![Week Card Icons](/images/sprint-week-icons.gif)
These three buttons appear at the top of each sprint card to help you manage your learning:

1. **▾ Weeks Button** - Expand/collapse sprint weeks
2. **<i class="fas fa-certificate"></i> Certificate Button** - Track progress and earn certificates
3. **<i class="fas fa-question"></i> Help Button** - Access this guide

## How to Navigate

### Completing Tasks
1. Click on any lesson or assignment title to open it
2. After completing the work, click the **circle icon** next to the item
3. The circle will change to a **check mark** to show completion
4. Your progress bar will automatically update

### Viewing Progress
The progress bar shows what percentage of the sprint you've completed. The format is:
- **X/Y Complete** - X items completed out of Y total items
- **Progress Bar** - Visual representation of your completion percentage

### Learning Goals
Each week displays **Learning Goals** - these are the key skills and concepts you'll master during that week.

### Earning Certificates
When you complete ALL tasks in a sprint:
1. Click the **Certificate Icon** button
2. View your progress in the modal
3. Click **View Your Certificate** to generate a personalized certificate
4. Download as PNG, print, or share on LinkedIn!

## Tips & Tricks

- **Track Your Progress**: The completion badges update in real-time as you check off items
- **Focus Mode**: Use the filter buttons (if available) to view only lessons, assignments, or incomplete items
- **Mobile Friendly**: The interface works great on phones and tablets - swipe to scroll through weeks
- **Save Progress**: Your completion status is saved automatically in your browser
