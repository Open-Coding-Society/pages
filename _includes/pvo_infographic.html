Poway Veterans Organization should redesign its website from an information site into a guided local support portal for veterans, families, volunteers, and donors.

Who Uses the Site

  • 🎖️
    Veterans in Need

    No guided help pathway — unclear eligibility, process, or timelines

  • 👨‍👩‍👧
    Families & Dependents

    No family-facing guide despite explicit mission inclusion

  • 🤝
    Volunteers

    Form-only page — no role types, time expectations, or examples

  • 💛
    Donors & Sponsors

    Impact is buried — no current stories or campaign options

  • 🎓
    Student Veterans

    Scholarship details exist but eligibility/deadlines unclear

  • 🏘️
    Community Partners

    Golf/event info isolated from broader partnerships story

Current Site Gaps

  • ✕Site organized by pages, not user needs
  • ✕Help pathway is not step-by-step
  • ✕Volunteers lack role context before the form
  • ✕No upcoming events visible (as of March 2026)
  • ✕Impact data is long-form archive, not scannable summary
  • ✕Newsletter frequency mismatch (monthly vs quarterly)
