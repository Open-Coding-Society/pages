About

The Poway Recovery Hub is designed to make addiction recovery resources easier to find and access. Many people who need help struggle to locate reliable information or community support. This platform centralizes local recovery services, meetings, and volunteer opportunities in one place.

The system also demonstrates core AP CSP concepts such as structured data for resources, search and filtering algorithms for meetings, and conditional logic to recommend services based on user needs.

Impact
Improves awareness of recovery programs, strengthens community support networks, and helps connect individuals and families with the resources they need to begin and sustain recovery.
