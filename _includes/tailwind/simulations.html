Mini-Game 2

Dice Rolling Simulator

Choose how many times to roll two dice. Watch the dice animate, then compare which sums appear most often.

HOW TO PLAY Enter the number of rolls, press run, and observe how repeated trials create a distribution. Sum 7 usually appears most often because it has the most combinations.
