About the Orchestra

The Poway Symphony Orchestra's mission is to make live classical symphonic music accessible to audiences and fellow performers across North County San Diego. Formed in 2004, the PSO has enriched the cultural and educational life of the region for over two decades.

View This Season Our Foundation Join the Orchestra

Conductor & Music Director

John LoPiccolo

Conductor and Music Director

Under John LoPiccolo's baton, approximately 65 musicians deliver performances at the high artistic level PSO audiences have come to expect. He leads the orchestra in programming beloved masterworks alongside exciting guest soloists each season, continuously raising the bar for classical music in North County.

The PSO Foundation

The Poway Symphony Orchestra Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization incorporated in Poway, established in 2015 with the sole purpose of supporting the orchestra. All donations to the Foundation are tax-deductible.

2025–2026 Concert Season

All performances take place at 4:00 PM at the Poway Center for the Performing Arts, 15498 Espola Road, Poway CA 92064. Free parking available.

Past

Dvořák in America

Sunday, November 16, 2025 · 4:00 PM

An all-Dvořák celebration of melody, heart, and heritage. Symphony No. 9 captures the grandeur of American landscapes and Dvořák's Bohemian soul — one of the most beloved symphonies in the repertoire.

  • Dvořák: Symphony No. 9 ("From the New World")
  • Dvořák: Cello Concerto — Cello (Qiele) Guo, soloist
Upcoming

Mendelssohn Classics

Sunday, March 22, 2026 · 4:00 PM

Elegance, drama, and Romantic-era brilliance. Features the beloved Violin Concerto in E Minor with guest violinist YuEun Gemma Kim, plus the bold "Reformation" Symphony.

  • Mendelssohn: Symphony No. 5 ("Reformation")
  • Mendelssohn: Violin Concerto in E Minor — YuEun Gemma Kim, soloist

An American Celebration

Sunday, May 31, 2026 · 4:00 PM

Celebrating the 250th anniversary of the United States with bold American voices — Copland, Hovhaness, Gershwin, and Bernstein — with pianist Dan Yu as guest soloist.

  • Copland: Fanfare for the Common Man
  • Hovhaness: Mysterious Mountain
  • Gershwin: Selections from Porgy and Bess
  • Bernstein: Selections from West Side Story
  • Dan Yu, piano soloist

Join the Orchestra

The Poway Symphony Orchestra welcomes qualified musicians of all ages and backgrounds. Our repertoire spans Classical, Romantic, and Contemporary works, performed at the highest artistic level. We are especially seeking additional string players in all sections.

  • Rehearsals: Monday evenings, 7:15–9:15 PM
  • Performances: Sundays at 4:00 PM
  • Dress rehearsals: Saturdays at 10:00 AM
  • Currently seeking string players in all sections
  • Musicians of all ages and walks of life welcome
