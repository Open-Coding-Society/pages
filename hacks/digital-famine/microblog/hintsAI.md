# Submodule 1 Quiz

1. A company develops an AI that can diagnose disease using medical images. It can outperform human doctors in this one task, but cannot drive a car or play chess. Which type of AI is this?  
**Hint:** Think about whether the AI is specialized in only one area or capable of doing many human tasks  
A) Super AI  
B) General AI  
C) Narrow AI  

---

2. An AI system that is vastly smarter than humans and capable of improving itself, creating new technologies, and making decisions beyond human understanding. Which type of AI is this?  
**Hint:** Think about which AI can surpass human intelligence  
A) Super AI  
B) General AI  
C) Narrow AI  

---

3. Researchers create an AI that can reason, plan, solve unfamiliar problems, and learn any intellectual task a human. It matches human intelligence across all areas. Which type of AI is this?  
**Hint:** This kind of AI would have the same capabilities as a human  
A) Super AI  
B) General AI  
C) Narrow AI
